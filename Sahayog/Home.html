Sahayog is a non-profit organisation providing clothes and stationaries to needy children in underdeveloped parts of Nepal. As of 2023, We have provided over 15,000 pieces of clothing, over 25,000 stationary items and food packages to 8000+ children in rural parts of Nepal such as Kailali, Baitadi, Bajura, Agrakhanchi and so on.
